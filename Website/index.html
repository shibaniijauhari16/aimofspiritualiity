Our Services

Tarot

Tarot can serve as a powerful tool for self-reflection, personal growth, and gaining insights into various aspects of our lives, your subconscious thoughts, emotions, and desires, gaining clarity and perspective on current challenges or situations, validation, affirmation, and reassurance, helping individuals trust their inner wisdom and make informed decisions.

Numerology

Numerology, the study of numbers and their mystical significance, offers insights into various aspects of life, guiding you to understand themselves and their paths better. By assigning meanings to numbers and analyzing patterns derived from birth dates, names, and other significant numbers in one's life, numerology can provide clarity and direction.

Vedic

Vedic astrology, rooted in ancient Indian wisdom and cosmic knowledge, offers profound insights into the complexities of life, guiding you towards fulfillment and harmony. Through meticulous analysis of planetary positions and their influence on human affairs, Vedic astrology provides a comprehensive understanding of one's personality traits, life events.

Palmistry

Palmistry, an ancient art dating back thousands of years, offers unique insights into one's character, potential, and destiny. Palmistry provides a window into a person's personality traits, strengths, weaknesses, and inclinations, hidden talents, patterns of behavior, and potential life paths, understand their unique place in the world.

Vastu

Vastu Shastra, an ancient Indian architectural science, integrates principles of design, layout, space arrangement, and spatial geometry to create harmonious living and working environments. Rooted in the Vedas, Vastu aims to balance natural elements—earth, water, fire, air, and space—to enhance health, prosperity, and well-being.

Counselling

Counseling is a professional practice aimed at helping individuals navigate personal, emotional, and psychological challenges through guided conversations and therapeutic techniques. It provides a safe and confidential space for clients to explore their feelings, thoughts, and behaviors, enabling them to gain insights and develop coping strategies.
